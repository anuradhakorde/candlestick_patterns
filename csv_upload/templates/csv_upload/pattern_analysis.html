Pattern Descriptions

Hammer Pattern

A reversal pattern with a small body and long lower shadow. Indicates potential upward price movement.

  • Small real body
  • Long lower shadow (2x body size)
  • Little to no upper shadow
Harami Pattern

A two-candlestick pattern where a small candle is engulfed by the previous large candle's body.

  • Large first candle
  • Small second candle within first
  • Indicates potential reversal
Doji Pattern

A pattern where open and close prices are very close, indicating market indecision.

  • Open ≈ Close
  • Small or no real body
  • Shows market uncertainty
